Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the facial analysis? +
Our facial analysis system achieves high accuracy rates using advanced computer vision algorithms. The system has been tested on thousands of images and validated against established beauty research. Results include confidence scores to help you understand the reliability of each measurement.
Is my uploaded image stored on your servers? +
No, we prioritize your privacy. Uploaded images are processed temporarily and automatically deleted after analysis. We do not store your images permanently or share them with third parties.
What image formats are supported? +
We support PNG, JPG, JPEG, and GIF formats. Images should be clear, well-lit, and show the face clearly. Maximum file size is 16MB for optimal processing speed.
